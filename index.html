Research Opportunity

Key Topics

  • Development of miniaturized biochemical and cell-based assays on a chip for drug discovery
  • Gene transduction on a chip to emulate human liver for toxicology screening
  • RNA interference on a chip to elucidate mechanisms of cellular responses for human toxicology
  • Development of an integrated chip platform to construct miniaturized 3D cell arrays containing a variety of primary human cells from different organs for toxicology screening
  • Construction of miniaturized 3D cell arrays containing primary human cancer cells from patient tissues for personalized cancer therapy

Minimum Qualification

  • 3.0 cumulative GPA
  • Experience in a lab environment
  • Current undergraduate student studying chemical/biomedical engineering
